slot[Z].smu[X].source.rangeY

This attribute configures the source range setting to be fixed for function Y.

Details

This setting configures the source fixed range setting for the specified source function.

The range setting is dependent on the maximum voltage level setting (see slot[Z].bank[X].maxvoltagelevel). When the maximum voltage level selects the 48 V rail rather than the 12 V range, the maximum voltage range is 48 V and the maximum current range is 100 mA. However, when the maximum voltage level selects the 12 V rail rather than the 48 V rail, the maximum voltage level is 12 V and the maximum current range is 250 mA.

Example

slot[1].smu[2].source.rangev = 12

Selects the 12 V range for channel 2 of the module installed in slot 1.
