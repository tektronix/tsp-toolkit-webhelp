slot[Z].smu[X].measure.rangeY

This attribute contains the positive, full scale value of the measurement range for voltage or current.

Type

TSP-Link accessible

Affected by

Where saved

Default value

Attribute (R)

Yes

Instrument reset
SMU reset
Recall setup

Not saved

Voltage: 200 mV
Current: 100 nA

Usage

rangeValue = slot[Z].smu[X].measure.rangeY

rangeValue

Range value:

  • MSMU200-2 voltage: 200 mV, 2 V, 6 V, 20 V, 200 V
  • MSMU60-2 voltage: 200 mV, 2 V, 6 V, 20 V, 60 V

    Current: 100 nA, 1 µA, 10 µA, 100 µA, 1 mA, 10 mA, 100 mA, 1 A, 1.5 A

Z

Module slot number

X

Module channel number

Y

The measurement function; v = voltage, i = current

Details

This attribute indicates the range used to measure voltage or current.

Example

print(slot[1].smu[2].measure.rangev)

Displays the present voltage measurement range for channel 2 of the module installed in slot 1.
