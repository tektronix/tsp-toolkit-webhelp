Details

This attribute configures the slew rate of the voltage source in volts per second.

The maximum slew rate is achieved when stepping from 0 V to ± full scale with a current limit >500 mA and a load >65 ohms.

Slew rate may be limited by the current limit. Set the current limit to greater than or equal to 500 mA to achieve the specified slew rate.

Slew rate may also be limited by the load, voltage level, and size of the voltage step. Adjust the slew rate setting to achieve optimal results under your conditions.

Example

slot[1].psu[2].source.slewratev = 5.0

Sets the voltage source slew rate to 5.0 V/s for channel 2 of the module installed in slot 1.
