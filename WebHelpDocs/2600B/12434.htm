Environmental conditions

Conduct your performance calibration procedures in a test environment with:

  • An ambient temperature of 18 °C to 28 °C.
  • A relative humidity of less than 70 percent unless otherwise noted.

Product specifications that are listed as 18 °C to 28 °C assume adjustment has been done at 23 °C. If the 2600B is adjusted at a different temperature, the specifications apply to ±5 °C of that adjustment temperature.
