LAN cable connection

The 2601B, 2602B, 2611B, 2612B, 2635B, and 2636B include two LAN crossover cables. Use one cable for the TSP‑Link® network and use the other cable for the LAN.

One cable is provided for the 2604B, 2614B, and 2634B for connection to the LAN. The TSP-Link® is not available on these models.
